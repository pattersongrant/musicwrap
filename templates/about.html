What is musicwrap?

  • Musicwrap is a platform for sharing and organizing music.
  • I made it as I thought sharing playlists would be more fun if they had greater customizability.


    • How do I use musicwrap?

  • Go watch the tutorial. It will explain a lot and give you new ideas.
