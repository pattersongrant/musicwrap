What is musicwrap?

Musicwrap is a platform for sharing and/or organizing spotify playlists.

I made it as I thought sharing playlists would be more fun if they had greater customizability.

How do I use musicwrap?

Go watch the tutorial. It will explain a lot and give you new ideas.

I encountered a bug!

Yes, Musicwrap is a very new platform that I just made for fun, so there may be many bugs.

Known issues include:

  • Can't use albums. (Try turning album into playlist?)
  • Naming is very restrictive, I know.
  • Mobile version is prretty much unusable.
  • Private/Incognito mode can cause bugs.
  • You must add at least one playlist before adding a background works.
  • You are unable to remove playlists after adding them.
  • Error codes have a few bugs.
  • Adding an invalid playlist link corrupts a wrap.
  • +Likely more!


I don't know what to make!

Try watching the demo for inspiration. This is a creative app, so keep trying until you make an enviornment that you want to share!
